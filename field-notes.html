Field Notes

Field Notes are fragments extracted from experience design, facilitation, and human systems in motion. They are not explanations. They are residues.

What remains after clarity has already happened.

01 experience design

People do not change when they understand. They change when their behaviour becomes impossible to continue.

02 group attention

Most misalignment is not disagreement. It is unsynchronised attention.

03 group dynamics

When a group becomes silent, something has been recognised but not yet made speakable.

04 system quality

The quality of a system is revealed in how quickly it adapts to discomfort.

05 language

Language is often used to delay perception.

06 conversation

In most conversations, what is not said is already doing the real work.

07 pressure

Pressure does not reveal truth. It removes alternatives.

08 attention

Attention is the first material of design. Everything else is secondary construction.

These notes are not meant to be read sequentially. They are meant to be encountered.
