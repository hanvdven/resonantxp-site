XP Catalog

Experiences are not delivered. They are entered.

Each XP is a designed situation where behaviour, interaction, and decision-making emerge in real time. Participants do not observe. They act inside the system.

XP-01

Leadership Under Pressure

A structured environment where participants must lead without full information, shifting conditions, and competing perspectives.

  • uncertainty as a design element
  • real-time decision making
  • visible leadership patterns

What emerges is not theory of leadership — but leadership as behaviour.

XP-02

Collective Intelligence Lab

A dynamic group system where no single participant has control, yet decisions must continuously be made.

  • distributed decision-making
  • interaction-based emergence
  • group awareness under pressure

Teams discover how intelligence is not located in individuals — but in interaction.

XP-03

Embodied Communication

A real-time interaction space where clarity, timing, and presence replace explanation as the primary communication tools.

  • non-verbal awareness
  • instant feedback loops
  • presence under uncertainty

Communication is not discussed — it is enacted.
