About

Han van der Ven

Experience designer · facilitator

I design situations. Not agendas. Not content. Situations — the kind that put people in contact with their own behaviour in real time, under conditions they did not fully control.

My background is in theatre, group dynamics, and systems thinking. Over the past two decades I have worked with leadership teams, management boards, and organisations navigating significant change — mostly in Belgium, the Netherlands, and Germany.

You cannot teach someone to lead under pressure.
You can design a situation where leadership either emerges — or doesn't.

What I have learned, slowly, is that the situation does most of the work. When the context is well designed, behaviour follows. When it is not, no amount of instruction or feedback makes up the difference.

The experiences I design are not simulations of real life. They are real life — compressed, made legible, returned to the people inside them. What you do in the room is what you do. There is nothing to role-play.

I work in Dutch, English, and German. I work with small groups and with large organisations. I work alone, and sometimes alongside colleagues whose practice I trust. I do not scale what should not be scaled.

Before this work, I spent years as a manager at a Big Four firm. Alongside, I founded the improvisation theatre troupes KaasWol and Commotie — winners of the Nederlands Theatersport Toernooi 2026 and the Flemish Improv Competition Kemphanen.
